Hey, thank you for visiting my site!

My name is Aleksandar Sultan and I'm 23 years old. I wrote my first program on October 14, 2020 and from that moment on, programming has become an integral part of my life on a daily basis. C # is the language in which I have mainly written code so far, but I am open at any time to new challenges in other technologies and in different parts of the professional field.

I've often imagined what I would answer to the question: "how I describe myself" and I came to the conclusion that I can hardly be sure which aspects of my character I describe as more positive or more negative. That is why I prefer judgment to be made by the other people. I describe myself as a person who adheres to discipline and accuracy, a positive and communicative personality. Sometimes I overdo with my persistence and meticulousness in solving a problem, but this is mostly due to my obsessive desire to get every detail to perfectionism.

What would my life be in 10 years? I imagine myself as a senior developer, interested in everything related to programming, not only in technical terms, but as a way of communicating both collegially and business-wise, as well as and friendly and emotionally, because I see in this profession a vocation that stimulates you to build your life on the most valuable life principles - trust and empathy.
